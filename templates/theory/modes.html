What Are Modes?

Take the 7 notes of a major scale. Now start on a different note but keep using only those same 7 notes. That's a mode.

Each starting point gives a different pattern of whole and half steps, which creates a completely different mood — even though you're using the exact same notes.

Modes aren't exotic or advanced — they're just the major scale viewed from 7 different angles. The brightness spectrum above shows how they range from Lydian (brightest) to Locrian (darkest).
